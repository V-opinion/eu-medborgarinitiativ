What is the difference between a flag and a banner?

While both flags and banners are ways of expressing ideas or beliefs, there is an important distinction between the two. A flag is usually used as a symbol or representation of a country, group, or organization. It is often flown from poles or from buildings and tends to be square or rectangular in shape. Banners on the other hand, tend to be larger and more flexible in size and design. They are often used for short-term campaigns, events, or other messages that may not require a physical flagpole.
